The Galactic Empire

The Galactic Empire, also known as the Empire, is the galactic, autocratic government that replaced the Galactic Republic in the aftermath of the Clone Wars.

The Empire secures victory by utilizing their overwhelming military force. While simultaneously exploiting mass-industrialization, as well as political and military espionage in order to expand their reach.
