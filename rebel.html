The Rebel Alliance

The Alliance to Restore the Republic, commonly known as the Rebel Alliance, Alliance, or the Rebellion, was a resistance movement formed by Bail Organa and Mon Mothma to oppose the reign of the Galactic Empire.

The Alliance and the cells before it were born from the overall discontent within the Empire who wished the Old Republic restored whose efforts were led by renegade members in the Imperial Senate.

As opposed to waging all-out war, the Alliance waged a war of attrition against the government, establishing in many cases small bases across the galaxy rarely controlling an entire planet.
